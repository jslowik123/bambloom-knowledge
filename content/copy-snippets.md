## On-Screen Text Styles

### For hooks (dramatic)
- ALL CAPS, large text
- White on dark background
- Red/dramatic color for "failure" text
- Quick fade or scale animation

### For features (informative)
- Title case or sentence case
- Warm brand colors
- Clean, readable
- Slide in from side or fade

### For CTAs (action)
- Bold, clear
- Pill-shaped button visual
- Green or brand primary color
- "Download Free" or "Link in Bio"

### For emotional (connecting)
- Italic or handwriting style
- Softer, smaller than hooks
- Warm colors (cream, sage, blush)
- Gentle fade animations
