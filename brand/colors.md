## Video Color Guidelines

- **Default to Warm Earth palette** for marketing videos (most recognizable)
- Use cream/beige backgrounds (`#F5EDE8`, `#E8DDD5`) — never pure white
- Green accents (`#7A9B6D`, `#81C784`) for positive/growth messaging
- Gold (`#FFD700`) for celebrations and achievements
- Red (`#FF6B6B`) only for "75 Hard" contrast (what we're against)
- Dark brown gradients for dramatic hooks
- Always warm and soft — never cold/clinical
